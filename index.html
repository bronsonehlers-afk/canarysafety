01 — The Challenge

THE PRICE OF LIMITED VISIBILITY

High-risk sites are dynamic, complex, and unforgiving. Workers are mobile and often isolated. Conditions change faster than procedures can adapt. Most sites lack the real-time awareness needed to prevent incidents before they escalate.

No Real-Time Worker Awareness
Supervisors can't see where their team is, what they're doing, or whether conditions have changed around them.
Delayed Emergency Response
Without immediate alerts, incidents escalate before anyone can react. Lost time costs lives.
Incomplete Incident Data
Without reliable field-level data, root-cause analysis is guesswork and regulatory exposure increases.
Higher Injury Severity
Limited visibility means incidents are caught later, when damage and injury are already more severe.
